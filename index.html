Frequently Asked Questions

Can someone else scan my QR Code?

No. Scan Your Boxes is fully secure and stranger can't scan the QR Code of your boxes.

Can I create as many box as I want?

Yes, you won't be limited on the number of boxes you can create, neither on the number of objects you want to put in them.
